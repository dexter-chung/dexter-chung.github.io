# Time Estimations

When our group was making the time estimations each person did their own estimations for their own tasks. In the process of making my time estimations, I measured it mostly by three factors, how difficult I perceived the task, is it something similar to what I have done before, and my overall knowledge surrounding the task. When trying figuring out how difficult a task is I usually look at what needs to be created whether that be pages, functions for buttons, UI, etc. For the second factor I basically just compare the task to previous projects that were already done and if a previous project has work that almost or does match than I know I can do the task a bit more faster as I got something to base it off. For the third factor it is basically just looking at what I know now and what I have experience with and comparing that with the task.

For whether or not making estimations provided any benefit, I think that it partially did. It helped me to gain a general idea of how long a certain task is gonna take. It also helped in planning what task to prioritize and what should be left for later.

# Tracking Actual Efforts

Tracking my actual efforts whether that be through just looking at the current time and comparing it to when I started or through a timer helped me quite a bit when doing my tasks. I helped me to keep pace and know when I spend too much time on something. It helped inform me on whether or not I underestimated or overestimated my ability to do tasks and so I can adjust my next time estimations accordingly. 

For how I tracked the time for my actual efforts, it was mostly either through best guesses or by using a timer in my phone. For the accuracy of my recorded times I think that overall they are about 75% accurate as sometimes I do a tasks that is worked on bit by bit on seperate days which could lead to some inaccuracies.


# What Would I Have Changed

One thing that I could definetly change about how I track time is start using the timer more often and less best guesses since using guesses is probably where most of the inaccuracies are coming from.
